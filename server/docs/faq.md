# NextStep Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is NextStep?
NextStep is a modern job matching platform that uses AI-powered semantic search to connect job seekers with relevant opportunities. We offer swipe-based job discovery, application tracking, and direct messaging with employers.

### Is NextStep free to use?
Yes, NextStep is free for job seekers. Employers may have different pricing tiers for posting jobs and accessing advanced features.

### How does the job matching work?
We use AI embeddings to understand your skills, experience, and preferences. When you search or browse jobs, our semantic search finds positions that match your profile, not just keyword matches.

## Account & Profile

### How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" and provide your email, phone number, and create a password. You can also sign up using Google OAuth for faster registration.

### Can I update my profile after creating it?
Yes! Go to your Profile page to update your name, email, phone, location, skills, photo, and resume at any time.

### How do I upload my resume?
On your Profile page, click "Upload Resume" and select a PDF, DOC, or DOCX file. Our AI will automatically analyze it and extract your skills.

### What happens when I upload a resume?
Our AI analyzes your resume and automatically extracts your skills. You can review and edit the extracted skills before saving your profile.

## Job Search & Applications

### How do I search for jobs?
Use the search bar on the Browse Jobs page. Our semantic search understands natural language, so you can search like "remote software engineer in Chicago" or "entry level marketing jobs."

### What is the swipe feature?
On the homepage, you can swipe right to apply to jobs or swipe left to pass. It's a quick way to browse through matched opportunities.

### How do I apply to a job?
Click on a job card to view details, then click "Apply" or swipe right. You'll need to have your profile completed before applying.

### Can I withdraw my application?
Yes! Go to "My Jobs," find the application, and click the "Withdraw" button. Note: This action cannot be undone.

### Can I reapply after withdrawing?
Yes, after withdrawing, the job will reappear in your job feed and you can apply again.

### How do I track my applications?
Visit the "My Jobs" page to see all your applications, their status (Pending, Interviewing, Offered, Rejected), and add personal notes.

### What do the application statuses mean?
- **Pending:** Your application has been submitted and is under review
- **Interviewing:** The employer wants to interview you
- **Offered:** You've received a job offer
- **Rejected:** The employer decided not to move forward

## Messaging

### How do I message an employer?
Go to the Messenger page, select a company from your applications, and start chatting. You can only message employers for jobs you've applied to.

### Can employers message me?
Yes, employers can message applicants who have applied to their jobs. You'll receive notifications when you have new messages.

### Are messages real-time?
Messages are checked periodically. Refresh the page or wait a few seconds to see new messages.

## For Employers

### How do I post a job?
Employers can post jobs through the Employer Dashboard. Fill in the job title, description, skills, location, salary, and benefits.

### How do I review applications?
Go to the Application Tracker to see all applicants for your jobs. You can update their status, add notes, and message them directly.

### Can I edit or delete job postings?
Yes, go to "Manage Job Postings" to edit or delete your active job listings.

### How do I update application status?
In the Application Tracker, click "Edit" next to an application, select a new status, and save. The applicant will receive an email notification.

## Technical Issues

### The chatbot isn't responding
The RAG chatbot requires ChromaDB to be running. If you're seeing errors, the service may be temporarily unavailable. Try refreshing the page.

### I can't upload my resume
Make sure your resume is in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format and under 10MB. If issues persist, try a different browser.

### Jobs aren't loading
Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the problem continues, the server may be experiencing issues.

### I forgot my password
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset your password via email.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Passwords are hashed, and sensitive data is protected.

### Who can see my profile?
Only employers whose jobs you've applied to can see your full profile. Your basic information is visible in the application tracker.

### Can I delete my account?
Contact support to request account deletion. We'll remove your data in accordance with privacy regulations.

## Contact & Support

### How do I get help?
Use the chat widget in the bottom-right corner to ask questions. Our AI assistant can help with most common issues.

### How do I report a bug?
Contact our support team with details about the issue, including what you were doing when it occurred and any error messages.

### Can I suggest new features?
Yes! We welcome feedback. Use the chat widget or contact support with your suggestions.
