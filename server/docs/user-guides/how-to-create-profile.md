# How to Create and Update Your Profile on NextStep

## Overview
Your profile is the foundation of your NextStep experience. A complete profile helps you get better job matches, makes you more attractive to employers, and enables all platform features.

## Why Your Profile Matters

### Better Job Matches
- Our AI uses your skills and location to find relevant jobs
- Complete profiles get 3x more relevant matches
- Your preferences guide our recommendations

### Employer Visibility
- Employers see your profile when you apply
- Complete profiles get 2x more interview requests
- Professional profiles make better first impressions

### Platform Features
- You need a complete profile to apply to jobs
- Messaging requires profile completion
- Resume analysis extracts your skills automatically

## Profile Requirements

### Minimum Required (to apply to jobs)
- ✅ First name
- ✅ Last name
- ✅ Email address
- ✅ Phone number
- ✅ Preferred location

### Recommended for Best Results
- ✅ Professional photo
- ✅ Resume (PDF, DOC, or DOCX)
- ✅ Skills list (at least 5 skills)
- ✅ Complete contact information

## Step-by-Step Profile Setup

### Step 1: Access Your Profile
1. Log in to NextStep
2. Click "Profile" in the navigation menu
3. You'll see the profile form

### Step 2: Add Basic Information

**First Name & Last Name**
- Enter your full legal name
- Use proper capitalization
- Example: "John Smith"

**Email Address**
- Use a professional email
- Check for typos
- This is how employers contact you
- Example: john.smith@email.com

**Phone Number**
- Include area code
- Use format: 123-456-7890
- Employers may call for interviews

**Preferred Location**
- Enter your city and state, or "Remote"
- Examples: "Chicago, IL", "Remote", "New York, NY"
- This affects job matching

### Step 3: Upload Profile Photo (Optional but Recommended)

**Why Add a Photo?**
- Makes your profile more professional
- Helps employers remember you
- Shows you're a real person

**Photo Guidelines:**
- Professional headshot preferred
- Clear, well-lit photo
- Appropriate attire
- Neutral background
- Smile and look approachable

**How to Upload:**
1. Click the edit icon (✎) on the profile image
2. Select a photo from your computer
3. Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
4. Photo appears immediately as preview
5. Click "Save Profile" to confirm

### Step 4: Upload Your Resume

**Why Upload a Resume?**
- Employers want to see your experience
- Our AI extracts your skills automatically
- Increases your chances of getting interviews
- Shows you're serious about job hunting

**Resume Guidelines:**
- PDF format preferred (also accepts DOC, DOCX)
- Keep it 1-2 pages
- Include: work experience, education, skills
- Use a professional template
- Proofread for errors

**How to Upload:**
1. Click "Upload Resume" button
2. Select your resume file
3. Wait for AI analysis (takes 10-30 seconds)
4. Review extracted skills
5. Edit skills if needed
6. Click "Save Profile"

**What Happens During Upload:**
- Our AI reads your resume
- Extracts skills automatically
- Adds them to your skills list
- You can review and edit before saving

### Step 5: Add Your Skills

**Why Skills Matter:**
- Used for job matching
- Employers search by skills
- More skills = better matches
- Shows your expertise

**How to Add Skills:**

**Method 1: Upload Resume (Automatic)**
1. Upload your resume
2. AI extracts skills automatically
3. Review the list
4. Remove irrelevant skills
5. Add any missing skills

**Method 2: Manual Entry**
1. Type a skill in the "Add a new skill" box
2. Click "Add Skill" button
3. Skill appears in your list
4. Repeat for all skills

**Skill Guidelines:**
- Be specific: "React.js" not just "JavaScript"
- Include tools: "Git", "Docker", "AWS"
- Add soft skills: "Team Leadership", "Communication"
- List certifications: "AWS Certified", "PMP"
- Include languages: "Spanish (Fluent)"

**How Many Skills?**
- Minimum: 5 skills
- Recommended: 10-15 skills
- Maximum: No limit

**Skill Examples by Role:**

**Software Engineer:**
- JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, SQL, Git, AWS, Docker, REST APIs, Agile

**Marketing:**
- SEO, Google Analytics, Content Marketing, Social Media, Email Marketing, Copywriting, Adobe Creative Suite

**Data Analyst:**
- SQL, Python, Excel, Tableau, Power BI, Statistics, Data Visualization, R, Machine Learning

### Step 6: Save Your Profile
1. Review all information
2. Make sure everything is correct
3. Click "Save Profile" button
4. You'll see a success message
5. Profile is now complete!

## Profile Sections Explained

### Personal Information
- **Name:** How employers will address you
- **Email:** Primary contact method
- **Phone:** For interview scheduling
- **Location:** Affects job matching

### Professional Information
- **Skills:** What you can do
- **Resume:** Your experience and education

### Privacy
- **Who sees your profile:** Only employers you apply to
- **What they see:** Name, contact, skills, resume
- **What they don't see:** Other applications, messages with other employers

## Common Profile Mistakes

### ❌ Incomplete Profile
**Problem:** Missing required fields
**Solution:** Fill out all required information

### ❌ Unprofessional Email
**Problem:** Using "partyguy123@email.com"
**Solution:** Use firstname.lastname@email.com

### ❌ Too Few Skills
**Problem:** Only listing 2-3 skills
**Solution:** Add at least 10 relevant skills

### ❌ Outdated Resume
**Problem:** Resume from 3 years ago
**Solution:** Upload your current resume

### ❌ No Photo
**Problem:** Generic avatar
**Solution:** Upload a professional headshot

### ❌ Vague Location
**Problem:** Just "USA"
**Solution:** Specific city or "Remote"

## Resume Analysis Feature

### How It Works
1. Upload your resume (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
2. Our AI reads the document
3. Extracts skills automatically
4. Adds them to your profile
5. You review and confirm

### What Gets Extracted
- Technical skills
- Programming languages
- Tools and software
- Certifications
- Soft skills mentioned

### What Doesn't Get Extracted
- Work experience (not added to profile)
- Education (not added to profile)
- Contact info (you enter separately)

### After Analysis
- Review the skills list
- Remove any incorrect skills
- Add any skills the AI missed
- Click "Save Profile"

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Upload Photo
**Check:**
- File is JPG, PNG, or GIF
- File size under 5MB
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache

### Can't Upload Resume
**Check:**
- File is PDF, DOC, or DOCX
- File size under 10MB
- File isn't password protected
- Try a different file

### Resume Analysis Not Working
**Try:**
- Wait 30 seconds (it takes time)
- Refresh the page
- Try a different resume format
- Upload a simpler resume

### Skills Not Saving
**Check:**
- Clicked "Save Profile" button
- Internet connection is stable
- No error messages
- Try refreshing and re-entering

### Profile Not Complete
**Verify:**
- First name ✓
- Last name ✓
- Email ✓
- Phone ✓
- Location ✓
- At least one skill ✓
