## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

**Method 1: Email Sign Up**
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a strong password
4. Provide your phone number
5. Click "Create Account"
6. Check your email for verification link
7. Click the verification link to activate your account

**Method 2: Google Sign Up**
1. Click "Sign Up with Google"
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant permissions
4. Your account is created instantly (no email verification needed)

### First-Time Setup

After creating your account:
1. **Complete Your Profile** (Required)
   - Add your full name
   - Verify your email and phone
   - Set your preferred location
   - Add your skills

2. **Upload Your Resume** (Highly Recommended)
   - Click "Upload Resume" on your profile
   - Select a PDF, DOC, or DOCX file
   - Our AI will automatically extract your skills
   - Review and edit the extracted skills
   - Save your profile

3. **Start Browsing Jobs**
   - Go to the homepage to see personalized recommendations
   - Or use "Browse Jobs" to search all available positions

## Job Search & Discovery

### Homepage Swipe Interface

The homepage shows personalized job recommendations based on your profile.

**How to Use:**
- **Swipe Right** (or click âœ“): Apply to the job
- **Swipe Left** (or click âœ—): Pass on the job
- **Swipe Up**: Skip for now (job may appear again later)

**Tips:**

**What You See on Each Card:**

### Browse Jobs Page

Search and filter all available jobs.

**Search Features:**
- **Natural Language Search**: Type queries like:
  - "remote software engineer in Chicago"
  - "entry level marketing jobs"
  - "high paying data science positions"
  - "part-time customer service near me"

- **Semantic Search**: Our AI understands context, not just keywords
  - Searches for "developer" also finds "engineer" and "programmer"
  - Understands synonyms and related terms
  - Matches your intent, not just exact words

**Search Results:**
- Shows number of jobs found
- Each job card displays key information
- Click any card to view full details
- Click "Apply" to submit your application

### Job Details Page

View complete information about a specific job.

**What's Included:**
- Full job description
- Required skills and qualifications
- Salary range
- Benefits package
- Work schedule (full-time, part-time, contract)
- Location(s)
- Company information
- Company website link

**Actions:**
- **Apply Now**: Submit your application
- **Back**: Return to previous page

## Applications

### Applying to Jobs

**Three Ways to Apply:**

1. **Swipe Right** on homepage
2. **Click Apply** on Browse Jobs page
3. **Click Apply Now** on Job Details page

**What Happens When You Apply:**
- Your profile and resume are sent to the employer
- Application status is set to "Pending"
- Job appears in your "My Jobs" page
- You can now message the employer
- Job is removed from your feed (no duplicate applications)

**Requirements to Apply:**
- Must be logged in
- Must have completed profile (name, email, phone, location)
- Must have added skills
- Resume is highly recommended but not required

### My Jobs Page

Track all your applications in one place.

**What You See:**
- Job title
- Company name
- Date applied
- Current status
- Personal notes

**Application Statuses:**
- **Pending**: Employer is reviewing your application
- **Interviewing**: Employer wants to interview you (check messages!)
- **Offered**: You received a job offer (congratulations!)
- **Rejected**: Employer decided not to move forward

**Features:**
- **Add Notes**: Click "Edit" to add personal notes about each application
- **Withdraw**: Click "Withdraw" to remove your application
- **Sort/Filter**: View by status or date applied

### Withdrawing Applications

If you need to withdraw an application:

1. Go to "My Jobs" page
2. Find the application you want to withdraw
3. Click the red "Withdraw" button
4. Confirm your decision (this cannot be undone)
5. Application is immediately removed

**When to Withdraw:**
- You've accepted another offer
- The job no longer interests you
- The location doesn't work for you
- You found a better opportunity

**Important Notes:**
- Withdrawal cannot be undone
- You can reapply to the same job after withdrawing
- Employer will see your application is no longer active
- You can withdraw at any status (Pending, Interviewing, Offered, Rejected)

### Status Updates & Notifications

**Email Notifications:**
You'll receive emails when employers update your status:

- **Pending**: Confirmation that your application was received
- **Interviewing**: Invitation to schedule an interview
- **Offered**: Job offer letter with details
- **Rejected**: Notification that they're moving forward with other candidates

**Checking Status:**
- Visit "My Jobs" page anytime
- Status updates appear in real-time
- Check messages for additional communication from employers

## Messaging

### Messaging Employers

**Starting a Conversation:**
1. Go to "Messenger" page
2. Click "New" button
3. Select a company from your applications
4. Type your message
5. Click "Send"

**Important:**
- You can only message employers for jobs you've applied to
- Messages are checked every 2 seconds for updates
- Employers can see your profile when you message them

**Message Features:**
- Real-time messaging
- Unread message badges
- Message history
- Timestamp for each message

**When to Message Employers:**
- Follow up on your application
- Ask questions about the role
- Respond to interview invitations
- Clarify job requirements
- Express continued interest

**Messaging Tips:**
- Be professional and courteous
- Keep messages concise
- Respond promptly to employer messages
- Proofread before sending
- Don't spam or send multiple messages in a row

### Reading Messages

**Viewing Conversations:**
1. Go to Messenger page
2. See list of employer contacts on the left
3. Unread messages show a badge with count
4. Click a contact to view conversation
5. Messages are marked as read after 2 seconds

**Message Indicators:**
- **Sent messages**: Appear on the right (blue)
- **Received messages**: Appear on the left (gray)
- **Timestamps**: Show when each message was sent
- **Unread badge**: Red circle with number of unread messages

### Employer Responses

Employers can:
- Reply to your messages
- Ask follow-up questions
- Schedule interviews
- Request additional information
- Send updates about your application

**Response Time:**
- Varies by employer
- Some respond within hours
- Others may take a few days
- Check messages regularly

## Account Settings

### Email Verification

**Why Verify:**
- Confirms your email is valid
- Enables password reset
- Receives application updates
- Required for full account access

**How to Verify:**
1. Check your email after signing up
2. Look for email from NextStep
3. Click the verification link
4. You'll see a success message
5. Return to NextStep and log in

**Didn't Receive Email?**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Click "Resend Verification" on login page
3. Wait a few minutes
4. Check email again

### Changing Password

**To Reset Password:**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click the link
5. Enter new password
6. Confirm new password
7. Log in with new password

**Password Requirements:**
- At least 8 characters
- Mix of letters and numbers
- Include special characters (recommended)
- Don't reuse old passwords

### Logging Out

**To Log Out:**
1. Click your name/profile in navigation
2. Click "Logout"
3. You'll be redirected to login page

**Security Tips:**
- Always log out on shared computers
- Don't save password on public devices
- Log out if you'll be away for a while

### Account Security

**Best Practices:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your password
- Log out on shared devices
- Keep your email secure
- Enable two-factor authentication (if available)
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### Deleting Your Account

To delete your account:
1. Contact support through the chat widget
2. Request account deletion
3. Confirm your identity
4. Your data will be removed per privacy regulations

**What Gets Deleted:**
- Your profile information
- Application history
- Messages
- Resume
- Account credentials

**What Employers Keep:**
- Applications you submitted (for their records)
- Messages sent to them

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Log In:**
- Check email and password are correct
- Verify your email address
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Jobs Not Loading:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in

**Can't Apply to Jobs:**
- Make sure you're logged in
- Complete your profile (name, email, phone, location, skills)
- Check if you've already applied
- Try refreshing the page

**Resume Upload Fails:**
- Check file format (PDF, DOC, DOCX only)
- Ensure file is under 10MB
- Try a different browser
- Convert to PDF and try again

**Messages Not Sending:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Make sure you've applied to the job
- Try logging out and back in

**Profile Won't Save:**
- Check all required fields are filled
- Ensure email format is valid
- Check phone number format
- Try refreshing and saving again

**Use the Chat Widget:**

3. Get instant AI-powered help
4. See relevant documentation sources

**Contact Support:**
