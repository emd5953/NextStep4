# How to Withdraw a Job Application

## Overview
You can withdraw your application from any job at any time through the "My Jobs" page.

## Steps to Withdraw

1. **Navigate to My Jobs**
   - Click on "My Jobs" in the navigation menu
   - You'll see a list of all your job applications

2. **Find the Application**
   - Locate the job you want to withdraw from
   - Review the job title, company, and application status

3. **Click Withdraw Button**
   - Click the red "Withdraw" button in the Actions column
   - A confirmation dialog will appear

4. **Confirm Withdrawal**
   - Read the confirmation message carefully
   - Click "OK" to confirm or "Cancel" to keep your application
   - **Note:** This action cannot be undone

5. **Application Removed**
   - The application will be immediately removed from your list
   - You'll see a success message
   - The job will reappear in your job feed if you want to reapply

## Important Notes

- **Cannot be undone:** Once withdrawn, you'll need to reapply if you change your mind
- **Employer notification:** Employers will see your application is no longer active
- **Status doesn't matter:** You can withdraw at any stage (Pending, Interviewing, Offered, Rejected)
- **Reapplying:** You can reapply to the same job after withdrawing

## When to Withdraw

Consider withdrawing if:
- You've accepted another offer
- The job no longer interests you
- The commute or location doesn't work
- You found better opportunities
- Company culture doesn't align with your values

## Related Features

- **My Jobs:** View all your applications
- **Application Status:** Track where you are in the hiring process
- **Job Search:** Find new opportunities after withdrawing
