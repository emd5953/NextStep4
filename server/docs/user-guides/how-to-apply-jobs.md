# How to Apply to Jobs on NextStep

## Overview
NextStep offers multiple ways to apply to jobs: through the swipe interface, browse page, or job details page. This guide covers all methods.

## Prerequisites
Before applying to jobs, make sure you have:
- ✅ Created an account
- ✅ Completed your profile (name, email, phone, location)
- ✅ Added your skills
- ✅ Uploaded your resume (recommended)

## Method 1: Swipe to Apply (Homepage)

### Steps
1. **Go to Homepage**
   - After logging in, you'll see the homepage with job cards
   - Jobs are personalized based on your profile and skills

2. **Review Job Card**
   - Each card shows: job title, company, location, salary, skills
   - Read the job description and requirements

3. **Swipe Right to Apply**
   - Swipe the card to the right, OR
   - Click the green checkmark button
   - You'll see a confirmation animation

4. **Application Submitted**
   - The job is added to your "My Jobs" page
   - Status is set to "Pending"
   - You can now message the employer

### Tips
- Swipe left (or click X) to pass on a job
- Jobs you pass on won't show up again
- You can only apply to each job once

## Method 2: Browse and Apply

### Steps
1. **Go to Browse Jobs**
   - Click "Browse Jobs" in the navigation menu
   - You'll see a searchable list of all available jobs

2. **Search for Jobs**
   - Use the search bar to find specific jobs
   - Try natural language: "remote software engineer in Chicago"
   - Our semantic search understands context, not just keywords

3. **Click on a Job**
   - Click any job card to view full details
   - Review the complete job description, requirements, and benefits

4. **Click Apply Button**
   - Click the "Apply" button at the bottom
   - Confirm your application
   - You'll be redirected to "My Jobs"

## Method 3: Apply from Job Details

### Steps
1. **Find a Job**
   - From homepage, browse page, or search results
   - Click on any job to open the details page

2. **Review Full Details**
   - Read the complete job description
   - Check required skills and qualifications
   - Review salary range and benefits
   - See company information

3. **Click Apply**
   - Scroll to the bottom
   - Click the "Apply Now" button
   - Confirm your application

4. **Application Submitted**
   - You'll see a success message
   - The job appears in "My Jobs"
   - You can now track its status

## After Applying

### Track Your Application
1. Go to "My Jobs" page
2. See all your applications in one place
3. Check status: Pending, Interviewing, Offered, or Rejected
4. Add personal notes to each application

### Application Statuses
- **Pending:** Employer is reviewing your application
- **Interviewing:** Employer wants to interview you (check messages!)
- **Offered:** You received a job offer (congratulations!)
- **Rejected:** Employer decided not to move forward

### Message the Employer
1. Go to Messenger page
2. Select the company from your applications
3. Start a conversation
4. Ask questions or follow up on your application

### Withdraw if Needed
If you change your mind:
1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Find the application
3. Click "Withdraw" button
4. Confirm (this cannot be undone)

## Common Questions

### Can I apply to the same job twice?
No, you can only apply once per job. If you withdraw, you can reapply.

### Do I need a resume to apply?
It's highly recommended but not required. Employers prefer applicants with resumes.

### How long does it take to hear back?
It varies by employer. Check your "My Jobs" page for status updates. Employers will update your status and may message you.

### Can I edit my application after submitting?
No, but you can update your profile at any time. Employers see your current profile when reviewing applications.

### What if I accidentally applied?
You can withdraw your application from the "My Jobs" page.

### How many jobs can I apply to?
There's no limit! Apply to as many jobs as you're interested in.

## Troubleshooting

### "You've already applied" error
You can only apply once per job. Check "My Jobs" to see if you already applied.

### Apply button is disabled
Make sure your profile is complete (name, email, phone, location, skills).

### Job disappeared after swiping
If you swiped left (passed), the job won't show again. If you swiped right (applied), check "My Jobs."

### Can't find a job I saw earlier
Use the search feature on Browse Jobs page. If you passed on it, it won't appear again.
